The Cream Day Era

The Cream Day Era saw me joining a pseudo band with a few of my friends. Containing of P Slimer, lil kc (the new name of Lil Yom), and Jimmy Frankfurt Da Rappa. We only really released one song as a trio, but there were a few songs leading up to the release of Greetings Loved Ones.
The idea with Cream Day was to make... freaky parodies.

Greetings Loved Ones (ft. Lil Kc & Jimmy Frankfurt Da Rappa) - Sep 13, 2024

The Official Release version of Greetings Loved Ones. Freaky j was cut, and the final member of Cream Day was featured; Jimmy Frankfurt The Rappa. This song actually had some effort put into it than any before. However, it was very freaky. Just ignore this...
This was released on BandLab - and later - Soundcloud, and I believe did get a music video.

Greetings Loved Ones (ft. lil kc & freaky j) - Aug 15, 2024

The original version of Greetings Loved Ones. Me and lil kc were up late at night again and we decided to make a song again. We decided to make a classical parody of California Gurls. It was pretty clapped. At this point it was just me, lil kc, and freaky j. Freaky j never ended up recording on this, but he will come back later, trust me...
This was released on BandLab.

Mary Had A Little Choad, Yo - Aug 05, 2024

This was the first song I ever released solo on BandLab. I had a midi keyboard and was messing around with it, trying to play some melodies. I started recording and playing Mary Had a Little Lamb and I messed it up a few times. But i persevered and after I finished recording, I decided to add drums. And after that, I added some vocals over it.
This was released on BandLab - and later - Soundcloud.

Song Of The Summer! (ft. lil kc) - Aug 14, 2024

To be honest I dont really know the story behind this. I just made some weird non-music soundscape of a concert with some crowd members being heard. See if you can pick them all out.
This was released on BandLab - and later - Soundcloud.
